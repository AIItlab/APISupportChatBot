Q: How do I cancel my booking?
A: To cancel your booking, please visit our website and go to "Manage Booking" section. Enter your booking reference and last name, then select "Cancel Booking". Please note that cancellation fees may apply depending on your fare type and timing. For bookings made within 24 hours, you may be eligible for a full refund.

---

Q: Can I select my seat after booking?
A: Yes, you can select your seat after booking through our website or mobile app. Go to "Manage Booking", enter your details, and click on "Select Seats". Please note that preferred seats may have additional charges. Seat selection is subject to availability.

---

Q: What documents do I need for international travel?
A: For international travel, you need a valid passport with at least 6 months validity from your travel date. Depending on your destination, you may also need a visa. Please check the embassy or consulate website of your destination country for specific visa requirements. Some countries also require proof of vaccination or health certificates.

---

Q: How early should I arrive at the airport?
A: We recommend arriving at least 2 hours before domestic flights and 3 hours before international flights. This allows sufficient time for check-in, security screening, and boarding procedures. During peak travel periods or if you have special requirements, please allow additional time.
